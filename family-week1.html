My Family Story

The story starts with my family's genealogy book, which is a book to decide all descendants’ second characters in name. A couple hundred years ago, my ancestor developed his own genealogy book and that really defined the family spirit of us- Ambitious. My family name is Chen, which means old/previous in Chinese. The second characters that this ancestor chose for us are all related to one meaning- to add upon. By putting them together, everyone in my family has a name meaning to behave better than the previous generation.

Personally I have no idea about my whole family history, only stories I remember are just related to my father and my grandfather. My grandfather, 陈-华-忠(means be more loyal), came from a small village. He experienced the cruel civil war and devastating cultural revolution in China when my father was young. He suffered a lot but he still decided to stay in the country when he had a chance to run to Italy. My grandfather said, “If no one stays, our homeland would never become a better place.”

My father, 陈-添-安(means to become safer), achieved the goal my grandfather put in his name. He dropped out from middle school due to the lack of money. After that, he decided to leave the village and do some business to stop the family from suffering from poverty, food shortage and other safety matters. He came to Beijing with an empty pocket in 1980 and finally settled after 10 years’ hard work. He successfully overcame tremendous difficulties and made it happen to create a super safe environment for me and my sister, which encouraged me a lot when I faced difficulties in my life.

Both my father and my grandfather didn’t fail the faith given by the ancestor in their names. Their braveness and ambition have helped our family arrive where we are now. I hope their spirits are not lost in my body, and it is the era for me to create my own stories.
